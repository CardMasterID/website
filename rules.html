Warning One

The first warning is given to members who engage in actions such as sending messages repeatedly, behavior that harms the harmony of the community, or using stickers that are not in accordance with applicable ethics and norms.

Warning Two

The second warning is given to members who use the platform for personal commercial purposes without permission or promote other groups or communities without the approval of the administrators.

Warning Three

The third warning is given to members who commit theft or plagiarism of content from others, make sensitive jokes related to politics, religion, or sexual orientation, which could cause controversy or conflict.

Warning Five

The Final Warning is given to members who share inappropriate content for the workplace (NSFW), express racist attitudes or comments, initiate or exacerbate internal drama, or insult individuals or groups.

Duration of Penalty

The penalties imposed on members who violate the rules may result in a kick or being expelled for several days according to the violated rule, for example, violating Warning 5 results in being expelled for 5 days.

Purpose of the Rules

These rules aim to ensure that the community remains safe, comfortable, and in accordance with the values upheld by the community administrators as well as other members.
